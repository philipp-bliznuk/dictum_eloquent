*ˈna-sᵊnt*
- **coming or having recently come into existence**
- **inchoate, initial**
	The actress is now focusing on her _nascent_ singing career.
	one of the leading figures in the _nascent_ civil-rights movement

	It was all for *naught*.

*ˈnȯi-səm*

	it's no fun having asthma and living in an area with _noisome_ smog

*ˌnän-di-ˈskript*

##### Noxious (adjective)
*ˈnäk-shəs*
- **disagreeable, obnoxious**
- **applies to what is both offensive and injurious to the health of a body or mind**
	mixing bleach and ammonia can cause _noxious_ fumes that can seriously harm you
	_noxious_ smog that for years has been encrusting the historic cathedral with soot
