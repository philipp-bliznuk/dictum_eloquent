##### Zeitnot (noun)
*ˈzaɪt-nət*
- **time trouble, time pressure, a situation where a player has little time to complete the required moves**
	Even thinking about a *zeitnot* causes me to blunder
	She found herself in *zeitnot* before the big game, unable to practice enough.

##### Zenith (noun)
*ˈzē-nəth*
- **the highest point reached in the heavens by a celestial body**
- **culminating point: acme**
	at the _zenith_ of her career as a dancer
	Ensure your target object is at least one-third of the way up from the horizon to the *zenith*.
